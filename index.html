Positives and Negatives

  • Remote Learning Is A Complete Success

    While there is a learning curve in shifting to remote learning, we found that there are also clear advantages. Overall, we found that students were more engaged and less distracted. In addition, technology tools also lend itself to scaling and better collaboration, which can be a plus in many scenarios.
